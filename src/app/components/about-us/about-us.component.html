🎯 Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

What drives the Surat Municipal Corporation every day

🎯

Our Mission

To provide transparent, efficient and responsive civic services to all citizens of Surat. Every complaint deserves a swift resolution and every citizen deserves to be heard.

🌟

Our Vision

To make Surat a Smart City where digital platforms empower citizens to actively participate in improving civic infrastructure and municipal services.

🤝

Our Values

Transparency, Accountability, Speed and Citizen-First approach guide every decision we make in managing and resolving public complaints.
